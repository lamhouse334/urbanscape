About Urbanscape

Welcome to our stage. Welcome to Urbanscape.

We are a Turnkey-service provider in design and execution, delivering excellent and quality projects for more than 2 decades. We offer residential and commercial solutions, specifically catering to your design needs, that works exclusively for you.

Armed with vast industry expertise, we create fantastic, long-lasting, and easy-to-maintain spaces that motivate and inspire. Whether the space is big or small, our client-centric approach offers you a stress-free experience while you see your space and vision come to life. Our eco-friendly and ergonomic solutions can help increase productivity, save energy, and add value to the property.

Our clear communication, organized approaches, meticulous planning, unparalleled discipline, steadfast ethics, and unmatched enthusiasm to achieve results that help us exceed our client’s expectations. We believe the experience should be as beautiful and poised as the impeccably executed design itself.

We are committed to elevating the standard of interior decor and design in India.

Mr. A.k Venugopal Pillai

With over 25 years of experience in Residential and Commercial projects across South India, founder AK Venugopal is a powerhouse of passion and expertise. He has in-depth knowledge and an unmatched understanding in a plethora of industry segments including Retail, Quick Service Restaurants, Office Spaces, Centralised Kitchens, and Premium Residential Projects. He has been associated with the commercial success of restaurants such as KFC, Pizza Hut, and Taco Bell. He has also collaborated with Architects based in Italy for a commercial venture of Italian Leather Goods in Bangalore.
Effortlessly donning several hats over the years, Mr. AK Venugopal has gathered immense knowledge in the areas of project planning, project documentation, execution, project management, and mall coordination which has augmented his success and attention to detail.
